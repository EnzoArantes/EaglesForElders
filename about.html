Eagles for Elders

Our Mission

At Eagles for Elders, we are looking to connect BC students with elderly people who are looking for any kinds of help and support. BC students can play games, go on walks, help cook and clean, and many more things!

Events

Community service events for Eagles for Elders are organized by the BC students themselves. If you are elderly and looking for a student, please contact us through the information provided on our Contact page. If you are a student looking to volunteer, please contact an elderly person through the Events page.
